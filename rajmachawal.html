Rajma Chawal - India’s Comfort Food

Preparation Time: 1 hour

Difficulty: Easy

Ingredients

  • Rajma
  • Rice
  • Onion, Tomato
  • Spices

Instructions

  1. Cook rajma.
  2. Prepare gravy.
  3. Mix and serve with rice.

Origin & History

Rajma Chawal is a staple dish of North India, especially Punjab, known for its comforting taste.
