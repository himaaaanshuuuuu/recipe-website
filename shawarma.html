Shawarma - Middle Eastern Classic

Preparation Time: 1 hour

Difficulty: Easy

Ingredients

  • Chicken/Meat
  • Pita bread
  • Garlic sauce
  • Vegetables

Instructions

  1. Marinate meat.
  2. Cook and slice.
  3. Wrap with sauce.

Origin & History

Shawarma originated in the Ottoman Empire and is now a globally popular dish.
