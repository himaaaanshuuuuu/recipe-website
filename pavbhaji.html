Pav Bhaji - Mumbai Street Delight

Preparation Time: 45 minutes

Difficulty: Easy

Ingredients

  • Potatoes
  • Tomatoes
  • Onions
  • Capsicum
  • Butter
  • Pav Bhaji masala
  • Pav (bread)

Instructions

  1. Boil and mash vegetables.
  2. Cook with butter and spices.
  3. Prepare thick bhaji.
  4. Toast pav with butter.
  5. Serve hot.

Origin & History

Pav Bhaji originated in Mumbai as a quick meal for textile workers. It later became one of India’s most iconic street foods.
