Daal Baati - The Pride of Rajasthan

Preparation Time: 1 hour

Difficulty: Medium

Ingredients

  • Wheat flour
  • Ghee
  • Dal
  • Spices

Instructions

  1. Prepare dough.
  2. Bake baati.
  3. Cook dal.
  4. Serve with ghee.

Origin & History

Daal Baati is a traditional Rajasthani dish developed in desert conditions where water was scarce.
